Health Analytics Solutions

Tekra Solutions's team is composed of multidisciplinary professionals with experience in the health industry, scientific research, statistical analysis, data mining, and other related fields. We focus on integrating health data with further data sources and using progressive geostatistical analysis methods to get the greatest information out of data. Tekra Solutions's health analytics services have been used to model vaccine supply and demand chains, identify populations at high risk of disease, and match local health resources to populations in need.

Healthcare analytics mentions to the use of vast quantities of collected data to deliver organizations with actionable insights. These insights are advanced through analytical disciplines to drive fact-based decision making. In turn, these choices improve arrangement, management, measurement and learning.

As healthcare administrations around the world are confronted to reduce costs, improve management with care teams, deliver more with less, and focus on refining patient care, analytics will be especially significant. Primary care physician and nursing shortages are requiring overworked specialists to be even more productive. Plus, fresh businesses entering the marketplace and new methods to healthcare delivery will growth competition in the industry. Building analytics capabilities can help healthcare organizations bind big data to create actionable insights that can be used by healthcare benefactors, hospital and health system leaders, and those in government health and human services to advance outcomes deliver value for the people they serve.

Key Services: Health Data Integration, Geostatistical Analysis, Vaccine Supply & Demand Modeling, Population Risk Identification, Resource Allocation Analytics, Healthcare Decision Support
