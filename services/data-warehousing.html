Data Warehousing Solutions

Tekra Solutions data warehousing services include all aspects of database design, development, testing, deployment, and maintenance, and are an integral part of most of our projects. Our team has extensive experience with leading platforms and technologies, large and small, and proprietary and open sources, such as MySQL, PostgreSQL, Oracle, dBASE, FoxPro, and Access. Tekra Solutions specializes in building enterprise data warehouses that automate workflows involving data compilation, cleanup, analysis, and reporting. Our data warehouse solutions integrate data from many sources in many formats, are extremely scalable, and can seamlessly integrate with existing systems.

Data warehouse (DWH) services help companies tool, improve or migrate their DWH solution to combine disparate data sources under one roof and enhance their decision-making.

Tekra Solutions' data warehousing services incorporate data modeling and architecting, data integration data governance, data warehouse migration services, enterprise data management services, performance, and managed services.

The process by which organizations adapt to collect data comprising transactional data from more than one data source such as the database of a transactional system, in a central location and then report that data in a gathered manner to business users in the group is called Data Warehousing. Tekra Solutions comprehends the complexity of this process and includes in its data warehousing services discrete and essential components to guarantee success for its clients.

Tekra Solutions offers easy access to complicated data and facilitates effective business analysis and business forecasting, resulting in effective decision-making. Flexibility is a vital tool that we offer to the executives to access data through queries.

Key Services: Data migration, processing, and conversion, Database management and administration, Project management, Database design and development, Life cycle planning, custom application development, Web hosting and cloud services
