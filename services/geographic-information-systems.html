Geographic Information Systems (GIS)

Tekra Solutions specializes in developing enterprise geographic information systems (EGISs) that enable organizations to be more successful. GIS not only permits for powerful data visualization; it permits for data relationships to be thoroughly analysed so that more informed and effective decisions can be made. Our EGIS services are best often provided alongside custom web application development and data warehousing to deliver complete solutions to our clients. Our team has knowledge working with many GIS platforms, including those offered by industry leaders Google, as well as other open source groups.

A geographic information system (GIS) is a system that creates analyses, manages and maps all types of data. GIS joins data to a map, incorporating location data (where things are) with all types of descriptive information (what things are like there). This offers a foundation for mapping and analysis that is used in science and almost every industry. GIS benefits users understand patterns, relationships, and geographic context. The assistances include improved communication and efficiency as well as better management and decision making.

Integrating maps and information using geographic information systems while keeping lower project costs is crucial to improve overall business processes for a varied range of industries including Defense, Construction, Telecommunications, Civil Engineering, Mining, Public Utilities, Transportation, Oil & Gas and Transport, among others. Initial from data collection to geological mapping or geospatial data representation to geospatial analysis, Tekra Solutions geographic information system services bring in both experience and expertise to help clients know the design functionality based on geographical data.
